Zorblax

The planet Zorblax is the only inhabited planet in the Antares Star System. Zorblaxians are peaceful and have a very scientific culture. They can be rude to strangers and travellers who do not bother to learn their language. Most major cities are in Zorblax's southern hemisphere, while the north is the primary source of agriculture.

We recommend travelling between November and March, when the crowds are lowest. Late winter offers the best deals on starflights and lodging.You muust visit the Museum of Squid-Monster Fossils. Situated in downtown Canto, this is the one place to go if your time on Zorblax is limited.

Naboo

An idyllic world close to the border of the Outer Rim Territories, Naboo is inhabited by peaceful humans known as the Naboo, and an indigenous species of intelligent amphibians called the Gungans. Naboo's surface consists of swampy lakes, rolling plains and green hills.



Naboo was pushed to the forefront of galactic politics as the birthplace of the Dark Lord of the Sith Sheev Palpatine, who served as its representative in the Senate of the Galactic Republic.
